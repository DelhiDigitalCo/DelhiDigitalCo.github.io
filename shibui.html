Shibui- an art of simplicity

Shibui is a sustainable brand working upon its journey to reach aesthetic fashion made out of eco-friendly and biodegradable fabrics taking you one step closer to the environment. They unleash creativity in every item they create bringing it to the simple and essential designs that are meticulously curated from recyclable fabrics

About The Project

Shibui partnered with DDC to strengthen its eCommerce platform and to bring awareness among the people towards its aesthetic and sustainable fashion brand through its official website.

The client’s requirement far exceeded our expectations on every front as they directly focussed on simple and minimal design along with some artwork, uniquely featured products, and pop-ups section on their website which directly influences their brand.
